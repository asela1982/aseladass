My Story

I am a Tableau Desktop Qualified Associate with experience in deploying data products using Python, JS, and SQL. I'm interested in creating, visualizing, programming, exploring, and learning.

I am presently living in Austin, USA pursuing a program in Data Analytics and Visualization at the University of Texas, Austin.

I was introduced to Data Analytics in 2014 when I was employed at Nations Trust Bank in Sri Lanka. One of my primary responsibilities was to establish a Tableau center of excellence to derive meaningful insights by visualizing the bank's data. Having had several years of experience working in Microsoft Excel and Access, I felt obsessed with Tableau and so did the whole bank.

As more and more people started using Tableau, the demand for Data Analytics grew. This is when I finally decided to take a ticket to USA, the hub for high-tech innovation and scientific development, to further enhance my Analytical skills. It was a tough decision to quit my job and say Goodbye to my parents, but I guess life is all about taking tough decisions for what you truly like to do.

Apart from reading for the program at UT, I maintain a technical blog in medium to share my experiences in Data Analytics. In general, I prefer tools that are used in the journalism scene like D3.js and are open-source. I am a bit of a perfectionist, and hence I have a collection of technical books to dive into if I encounter a snag along the way. I also try my best to avoid using stackoverflow and google for answers, and instead prefer to refer official documentations.

My long terms aspirations are to continue putting more time and effort into sharpening my statistical thinking, programming, and mathematical skills in order to make better data products.
