My Story

Data Analyst with experience in deploying full-stack web applications using Python, JS, and SQL. I'm interested in visualizing, coding, exploring, and learning.

I am presently living in Austin, USA pursing a program in Data Analytics and Visualization at the University of Texas, Austin.

I was introduced to Data Analytics in 2013 when I was employed at Nations Trust Bank in Sri Lanka. One of my primary responsibilities was to establish a Tableau center of excellence to derive meaningful insights by visualizing the bank's data. Having had several years of experience working in Microsoft Excel and Access, I felt obsessed about Tableau and so did the whole bank.

As more and more people started using Tableau, the demand for Data Analytics grew. This is when I finally decided to take a ticket to USA, the hub for high-tech innovation and scientific development, to further enhance my Analytical skills. It was a tough decision to quit my job and say adios to my parents, but I guess life is all about taking tough decisions for what you truly like to do.

Apart from reading for the program at UT, I maintain a technical blog in medium to share my experiences in Data Analytics. In general, I prefer tools that are used in the journalism scene like D3.js and are open-source. I also make every effort to improve my statistical thinking skills.I am a bit of a perfectionist and hence I have a collection of technical books to dive into if I encounter a snag along the way. I also try my best to avoid using stackoverlow and google for answers, and instead prefer to refer official documentations.
