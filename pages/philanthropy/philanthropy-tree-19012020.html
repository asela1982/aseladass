Project Name: Plant-a-Tree & Help-a-Needy

Project Location: Anuradhapura District - Sri Lanka
Project Date: 19 January 2020

Yet another project got successfully completed thanks to all those who made those generous contributions. Unlike the previous projects, in addition to supporing families in need of assistance, we wanted to do something different this time - we planted a mango tree at each of the families we visited. We asked the kids to take good care of the trees in rememberence to the support they had received.

We visited close to 8 families in the Anuradhapura district in places like Rambewe - Diviyadabandawewa, Ataveeragollawa - Paddikgama, Madawachiya to provide dry rations and financial scholarships.

Genrous Contributors:

  • Tharaka De Alwis
  • Nilanga de Silva
  • Ashani Senaratne
  • Manilka Leanage

It is extremely difficult to put to pen those many thoughts pondering in my mind about the experiences I had on that day witnessing it first-hand the hardships these families have to endure on a daily basis. I felt greatful and happy to be part of a bigger cause to help the humanity in need to the best of my ability.
